Chronospace Dreamscape! is a psychological horror story that tells the tale of three devout Internet hackers who attempt to start a revolution against a maniacal, greedy company near the turn of the millennium in 1999.

Leap through chatrooms, explore the depths of TalkCity topics, and evade detection as the story of Wade "wadebraid" Harris, Wallace "wallpaste" Clark and Betty "bettyboop" Kristoff unfolds in all of its uncanny, tongue-in-cheek and unsettling 'glory'.
